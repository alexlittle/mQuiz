When you have studied this session, you should be able to:

1.1 Define and use correctly all of the key words printed in bold. (SAQ 1.1)

1.2 Explain the importance of postnatal care in terms of when most mothers and newborns die, and the main causes of these deaths. (SAQ 1.2)

1.3 Briefly describe the main physiological changes in the mother and newborn in the postnatal period. (SAQ 1.3)

1.4 Describe the main danger signs in the postnatal mother and newborn. (SAQ 1.3)

1.5 Explain the importance of community involvement in postnatal care and describe how you would establish partnerships with gatekeepers in the community. (SAQ 1.2)

Kufikia mwisho wa masomo ya kipindi cha kwanza, ni sharti uweze;

1.1 Kuleza na kutumia vyema majina yote ambayo yameandikwa katika rangi nyingi.

1.2 Kufafanua umuhimu wa huduma baada ya kujifungua wakati mwingi kina mama na watoto waliozaliwa hufa na sababu kuu za kusababisha vifo hivi.

1.3 Kwa kifupi kujadili mabadiliko ya kiakili kwa mama na mtoto katika huduma baada ya kujifungua.

1.4 Kuelezea hatari kuu katika kujifungua mtoto.

1.5 Kuelezea umuhimu wa jamii kujiunga na huduma baada ya kujifungua na kujadili vipi mtu anaweza kuanzisha ushirikiano na wasaidizi katika jamii.
