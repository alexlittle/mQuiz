In Study Session 11, you have learned that:

  1. There are different feeding recommendations for children according to their age.
  2. Infants born to HIV-positive mothers should be exclusively breastfed for six months and then given complementary foods as well as breastmilk.
  3. There are some common feeding problems in infants and children, and with some simple support and advice from you, mothers can usually overcome these difficulties.
